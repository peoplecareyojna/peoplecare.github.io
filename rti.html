RTI Help

People Care International Organization has introduced a department which works for people who are unable to fetch information under the “Right to Information Act, 2005”.

There are people who lack sources for information, members of this unit gratuitously assist such people to exercise their RIGHT to INFORMATION guaranteed to them. The only motive of the team or the organization is to “help the needy”. There is no fee or charge taken from the people.

The People R.T.I. Activities Team visions to create a responsive environment for every person that promotes overall development of the individual as well as society as a whole.

WHAT IS RTI ACT, 2005?

The Right to Information Act, 2005 was approved on 22.06.2005 and was commenced on 12.10.2005 . The aforesaid act was enacted by the Parliament of India and is applicable on the whole of India including Jammu Kashmir.

The Right to Information Act, 2005 is an act of the Parliament of India which sets out the rules and procedures for the citizens of India regarding their RIGHT TO INFORMATION. Under the provisions of the said act, the citizens of India may request information from a public authority , including Government of India whether central or state. The act also requires every public authority to publish certain categories of information so that the citizens need minimum recourse to request for information formally.

RTI Help

The type of information which may be obtained is defined under the Section 2(f) of the RIGHT TO INFORMATION Act of 2005 as any material in any form , including records, documents , memos, mails, opinions, advices , press releases, circulars , orders , log books, contracts, reports, papers, samples, models, data materials heldinanyelectronicformand.

  • In case of a CENTRAL GOVERNMENT DEPARTMENTS, INR 10.00 by way of cash under proper receipt, demand draft, bankers cheque, Indian postal orders, money order or cash deposited at designated cash offices.
  • In case of STATE GOVERNMENT DEPARTMENTS, particularly in the state of Maharashtra, INR 10.00 by way of cash under proper receipt, demand draft , money order, banker’s cheque.

BENEFITS OF PEOPLE RTI ACTIVITIES TEAM

  • The information will become easy to understand as it will be free from jargons of the official language.
  • The information is easily accessible.
  • Most importantly, this lessens the discrimination as information is provided uniformly to every person assisting for it. Everybody has equal access to the information.
  • The team becomes the platform of overcoming communication barriers of people with the administration department which brings more efficiency and accountability.
