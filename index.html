People Care Provide Worldwide Charity Service
‘People Care’ is a non-profit Charity Organization with more than 27 branches in India, People Care has transformed lives in every dimension spoken. From eliminating the root problems of education by providing free education and meals to providing special guided education to the deaf and dumb. From teaching life skills to the visually impaired to the professional skill development of the differently-abled.

‘People Carer’ was established in the year 2017 with a fistful of flour and to serve the patients belonging to the physically and economically disadvantaged sections of the society through the free-of-cost corrective surgeries of patients suffering from polio and other congenital disabilities. The headquarters of the organization is at Delhi city in the state of New Delhi, India.
