PEOPLE SMART VILLAGE

People Care International Organization work for the uplifment of poor village, rural development and to unleash their potential and to secure the disadvantages village people face in their growth. People Smart Village is a department of PCIO which is involved to introduce village people with every Latest technologies that can help people for living easy life.

  • Every GP/Ward has functional water conservation and harvesting structures.
  • Every GP/Ward has functional Information Center, Computer Lab, & Mee Seva Center.

OBJECTIVES OF PEOPLE SMART VILLAGE

  • Homes for all- with acces to toilet, safe drinking water and regular power
  • End all preventable maternal deaths and infant deaths.
  • Zero school drop outs of boys & girls.
  • Malnutrition free (children below 9)
  • No girl child marriage (girls below 18 years of age).
  • Every village household has a functional bank account/ PM Jhan dhan bank account.
  • Every GP/Ward has a green trees plantation all over its geographic boundaries.

AIMS OF SMART VILLAGE

  • Homes with access to toilet, safe drinking water and regular power.
  • A Smart Village knows all information about its citizens, available recourses, applicable services and schemes.
  • Maintain its Identity , Culture and Heritage.
  • It works towards Revenue generation.
  • Providing good basic health facilities.
  • Has functional solid/liquid waste management system.
  • Good facilities for Domestic animals like dogs & cattle : dispensaries, pond, veterinary hospitals and vets.
  • Awareness and implementation of new technologies in village, farms & nearby places. e.g. Drip Irrigation, Solar Panels Lighting System.

MODEL OF SMART VILLAGE

  • Education facilities- Smart class rooms, Computer lab.
  • Education to public- awareness of cleanliness, family planning, Agriculture methods.
  • Health support- Tele meds(online medicine & online diagnosis), Doctors on wheel (Mobile medical).
  • Efficient public transportation system.
  • Improving sanitation conditions- building toilets.
  • Rain water harvesting.
